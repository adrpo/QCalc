QCalc.UsersGuide.Glossary

Below are some terms used in QCalc. All of the terms have the usual definitions except for specific.

Extends from Icons.Information (Icon for general information packages (from MSL 3.2.1)).

Package Content

Name Description
'fluidity' (noun) reciprocal of dynamic viscosity [L T M-1]
'lambda unit' An invertible function that maps a number to a quantity and vice versa
'linear unit' A reference quantity that is used via multiplication to map a number to another quantity
'lineic' adjective that indicates the quotient of the following quantity and its associated length [× L-1]
'massic' adjective that indicates the quotient of the following quantity and its associated mass [× M-1]
'quantity' "property of a phenomenon, body, or substance, where the property has a magnitude that can be expressed as a number and a reference" [JCGM2012]
'specific' adjective that indicates the quotient of the following quantity and its associated chemical amount [× N-1]
'unit' A reference entity that is used to map a number to a quantity and vice versa
'volumic' adjective that indicates the quotient of the following quantity and its associated volume [× L-3]
